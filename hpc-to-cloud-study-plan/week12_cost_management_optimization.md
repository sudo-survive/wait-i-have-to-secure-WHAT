# Week 12: Cost Management and Optimization
## From Fixed Infrastructure Costs to Variable Cloud Economics

Complete week covering FinOps practices, cost optimization strategies, resource right-sizing, Reserved Instances, and cost governance for scientific computing.

---

## Learning Objectives

- Understand cloud cost models vs. fixed HPC infrastructure costs
- Implement FinOps practices and cost governance
- Apply cost optimization strategies and automation
- Design cost allocation and chargeback models
- Monitor and optimize resource utilization
- Create cost-aware architectural decisions

---

## Key Topics

### Day 1: Cloud Cost Fundamentals
- Cloud pricing models and cost drivers
- Cost monitoring and analysis tools
- Budget management and alerts
- Cost allocation and tagging strategies

### Day 2: Resource Optimization
- Right-sizing instances and services
- Reserved Instances and Savings Plans
- Spot instances for batch workloads
- Auto-scaling for cost efficiency

### Day 3: FinOps Implementation
- Cost governance and policies
- Chargeback and showback models
- Cost optimization automation
- Cross-functional cost management

### Day 4: Storage and Data Costs
- Storage class optimization
- Data lifecycle management
- Data transfer cost optimization
- Archive and backup cost management

### Day 5: Continuous Optimization
- Cost optimization automation
- Regular cost reviews and analysis
- Cost-aware development practices
- Long-term cost planning and forecasting

---

## Deliverable

Complete FinOps strategy and cost optimization plan with governance policies, automation tools, and continuous improvement processes for scientific computing workloads.
